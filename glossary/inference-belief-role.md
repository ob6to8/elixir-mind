# inference (belief role)

In a belief graph, a belief **derived from other beliefs** rather than asserted
directly — a genuine leap licensed to exceed its premises ("apples reproduce,"
from "apples are fruits" + "fruits reproduce"). The counterpart to an
[attestation](/glossary/attestation.md), and the node an
[ungrounded inference](/glossary/ungrounded-inference.md) check targets: every
inference must resolve transitively to at least one attestation. Corresponds to
the third of Composable Beliefs' four operations. Distinct from
[entailment](/glossary/entailment.md) (the formal support relation an inference
edge claims) — this is the *node*, that is the *edge test*.

# Goal Structuring Notation (GSN)

The dominant graphical standard for writing an
[assurance case](/glossary/assurance-case.md): goals (claims) decomposed via
explicit strategies down to solutions (evidence), scoped by
context/assumption/justification annotations, with SupportedBy carrying
inferential-or-evidential support and InContextOf carrying scoping.
Standardized by the GSN Community Standard (2011; v3 2021). Canonically
defined in
[assurance cases and GSN](/knowledge-management/argumentation/assurance-cases-and-gsn.md),
with verbatim element definitions in the
[standard capture](/knowledge-management/argumentation/gsn-community-standard.md).

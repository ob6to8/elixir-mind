# assertion graph

The DAG runs from any claim back to its origins. Its machinery (shell tooling, schema, generated index) was deleted on 2026-07-11; what waits under `deprecated/` is content only — sources and assertions — where assertions map roughly onto the `claim` type and the [verified-by](/beliefs/glossary/verified-by.md) evidence machinery.

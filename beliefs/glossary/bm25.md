# BM25

The "rarity" factor is inverse document frequency. Having no notion of meaning is what keeps it cheap and deterministic (its lower neighbor is grep) and what caps it: vocabulary mismatch is the failure mode that demands [embeddings](/beliefs/glossary/embeddings.md) and [semantic search](/beliefs/glossary/semantic-search.md).

# provenance

Distinct from `resource`, which is a canonical asset URI: provenance is the origin of the statement, not a link to the asset. The orthogonality means checking a statement never rewrites where it came from. Defined by the [frontmatter-schema policy](/meta/policy/frontmatter-schema.md).

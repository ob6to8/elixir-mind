# generative model

Because it captures the full distribution — over inputs and, when present, labels — it can also fill in or denoise partial inputs, not just synthesize from scratch. A [deep belief network](/beliefs/glossary/deep-belief-network.md) is a generative model; so are VAEs, GANs, and diffusion and autoregressive models.
